1. Think in probabilities, not right and wrong. When making a decision, always think about how 
likely it is that a certain path will lead to the desired outcome. This might sound obvious at first,
but it really isn’t. There are times when multiple paths look like they might achieve the same 
goal - it’s good to take a step back and think about the likelihood of each.
